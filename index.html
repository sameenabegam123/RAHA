Our Evolution

From Small Beginnings to Industry Leadership

Founded in 2015 with a vision to accelerate India's clean energy journey, Raha began as a small solar–wind integrator focused on bridging technology with practical project delivery. In the early years, we built our foundation on wind farms and small-scale solar projects in Tamil Nadu, establishing credibility through on-time execution and reliable engineering.

As India's energy landscape expanded, so did we. From a niche integrator, Raha evolved into a full-service EPC and project development firm, taking projects from land identification and acquisition to design, construction, and grid integration. Along the way, we added capabilities in power evacuation, storage solutions, and O&M services, ensuring our clients had a single partner for their entire renewable journey.

Today, Raha stands as a developer of utility-scale renewable infrastructure with a growing presence in industrial and distributed solar. From concept to commissioning, we continue to manage every step with precision — so our clients can focus on scaling their businesses, not solving gridlocks.

Our Leadership

Experience Meets Execution

At Raha, experience meets execution. Our leadership team consists of renewable energy veterans and infrastructure experts with deep domain knowledge in solar PV, wind turbine integration, and regulatory compliance. Their collective focus: delivering projects that perform — technically, financially, and environmentally.

N. Venkatesan

Founder & CEO

Mr. N. Venkatesan brings over 30 years of hands-on experience in India's renewable energy sector. He began his career in 1994 as a Project Engineer at NEPC Micon Ltd, one of the country's early wind energy pioneers, where he developed a strong foundation in technical operations and system reliability. In 1997, he joined KKSK Group as General Manager, leading strategic investments in non-conventional energy and driving significant business expansion. With this depth of expertise, he founded Raha Renewable Technology to make clean energy solutions more accessible, efficient, and commercially viable. Under his leadership, the company has grown from a small EPC outfit into a trusted turnkey developer of solar and wind infrastructure projects across India.

Satheeshkumar

Chief Project Director

Mr. Satheeshkumar brings over 15 years of proven experience in the renewable energy industry, having held senior technical and project management roles at globally recognized companies such as Gamesa, Vestas, and Enercon. His expertise spans the entire project lifecycle—from feasibility assessments and micro-siting to execution, grid integration, and post-commissioning support. At Raha Renewable Technology, he oversees the implementation of multi-megawatt solar and wind projects with a focus on operational excellence, regulatory compliance, and cost optimization. His core strengths include site and infrastructure planning, vendor and contractor coordination, quality control and HSE compliance, transmission line execution, substation integration, and on-site stakeholder engagement. Known for his meticulous approach and deep field knowledge, Mr. Satheeshkumar ensures every project is delivered with technical precision, minimal risk, and maximum long-term value.

Complete Energy Ecosystems From Concept to Commissioning

At Raha Renewable Technology, we don't just build projects — we deliver complete energy ecosystems.   • Site & radiation mapping
  • Land acquisition support
  • Layout and technical design

EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction)

With a deep bench of engineers and construction specialists, Raha executes utility-scale EPC contracts with speed and precision. We optimize cost, timelines, and quality to maximize performance and ROI.

Scope of Work:

  • System design & engineering
  • Procurement of PV modules, turbines, and BOS
  • Civil and electrical works
  • Erection, installation, and commissioning

Regulatory & Permitting Support

We navigate the maze of licenses, permissions, and documentation for you. Our in-house compliance team liaises with DISCOMs, government agencies, and statutory bodies to ensure seamless approvals and zero delays.

Coverage:

  • CEIG approvals
  • Grid interconnection
  • Net metering facilitation
  • Environmental and land clearance
  • Power purchase agreements (PPAs)

Operation & Maintenance (O&M)

We ensure your renewable energy assets perform at their best throughout their lifecycle. Our expert O&M team delivers proactive monitoring, timely interventions, and performance optimization to guarantee maximum uptime and energy yield.

Coverage:

  • 24/7 remote monitoring & diagnostics
  • Preventive maintenance (inspection, cleaning, component testing)
  • Corrective maintenance (fault detection, repairs, replacements)
  • Performance optimization & yield analysis
  • Spare parts & inventory management
  • Warranty & compliance support
  • Detailed reporting & analytics
